About AI Healthcare Assistant

Our Mission

To provide accessible, AI-powered health information that empowers people to better understand their symptoms and make informed decisions about their healthcare. We combine cutting-edge machine learning with medical knowledge to deliver accurate disease predictions.

How It Works

Our system uses advanced machine learning algorithms trained on over 246,000 medical records covering 754 different diseases and 377 symptoms. The AI analyzes the relationship between symptoms and diseases to provide predictions with up to 70% accuracy.

Key Technologies

  • Machine Learning: Random Forest classification with 70.64% accuracy
  • Data: Trained on 246,945 patient records with balanced class weighting
  • Smart Duration Analysis: Adjusts predictions based on symptom duration (acute vs chronic)
  • Personal Profiles: Tracks health history and provides personalized risk assessment
  • Modern Web Interface: Clean, intuitive design for easy symptom searching

Accuracy & Limitations

While our AI achieves 70.64% accuracy on test data, it's important to understand:

  • ✓ Our model is trained on real medical data
  • ✓ Duration-based adjustments improve accuracy for acute vs chronic conditions
  • ✗ This is NOT a substitute for professional medical diagnosis
  • ✗ Always consult with a healthcare professional for proper treatment
  • ✗ Rare diseases may not be properly identified
